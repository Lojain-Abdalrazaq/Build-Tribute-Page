Here's some information about Agatha Christie! Enjoy It!:

- 1890-Born in Britain, Devon.
-Christie was born into the wealthy upper-class Devon family in Torquay. Before marriage and setting up a family in London, she served at Devon Hospital during World War I.
-Best known for writing 66 detective novels and 14 short stories, she also wrote the world's longest play.
-She is very successful in writing and because of that she entered the Guinness Book of Records, which she sold, reaching 2 billion.
-Agatha Christie died on January 12 of 1976 at the age of 85.
